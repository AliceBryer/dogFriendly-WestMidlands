EATING OUT

The Horns Inn, Slitting Mill, Rugeley

01889 577608

The Horns Inn is a family friendly pub, nestled in the heart of Cannock Chase. A great menu with proper pub classics means there is something for everyone. Dogs are allowed in the bar area and this can become crowded at peak times but you can book ahead. All the staff give you a fuss, and there is ample biscuits & water available.

Visited On: Frequently

Stone Roses Bar, York City Centre

Dubbed as "probably the best indie bar in the north" , the Stone Roses bar is great for a drink whilst being surround by memoribilia and for listening to iconic indie tunes. Perfect for taking a break from shopping. Dog-friendly and no need to book, would recommend day times as can get very busy on evenings with drinkers. They don't serve food so have lost a paw.
